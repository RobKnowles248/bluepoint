Climbers have been able to log outdoor routes they have completed for years on sites such as 8a.nu and UKClimbing. However, until now (we think), there has still been nowhere for them to log successful belays of other climbers on routes. Bluepoint has fixed this injustice by providing a place for climbers to log their most valuable of achievements: Bluepoints
